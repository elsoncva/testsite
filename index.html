About Us

Empowering Lives Through Compassionate Excellence

Welcome to A Touch of Mercy, LLC, where compassion meets excellence in service. Founded by Mildred Rivers in 1998, our organization embodies a legacy of dedication, integrity, and unwavering commitment to making a positive impact in the lives of individuals and communities.

Explore our comprehensive range of services designed to cater to diverse needs and foster holistic well-being. From reliable transportation solutions to compassionate behavioral health services and nurturing educational programs, we are here to support you every step of the way.

Our Services

Discover The Comrehensive Services

Join us in our mission, where compassion meets action, and experience the touch of mercy
that defines our legacy. Together, we can make a difference.

Transportation Services
We offer Non-Emergency Medical Transportation to clients throughout the Shreveport-Bossier region is a crucial service for those without personal transportation. Our professional driving team is committed to safety while providing efficient routing and communications to pick up and deliver our precious clients to and from their destinations.
Behavioral Health Services
A team of professionals committed to providing top-notch, quality mental health support, tailored to each individual’s treatment plan. There are no cookie-cutter solutions to mental health issues, therefore ATOM has developed a proven assessment strategy that clarifies each individual’s needs and explores and determines the most effective course of action for successful treatment.
Personal Care
New Horizon Healthcare Solutions, LLC offers in-home care for those who need assistance with activities of daily living. Our professional caregivers are fully trained and provide tasks such as transfer assistance, personal hygiene, light housekeeping, meal preparation, medication reminders, and accompany our clients to their medical appointments.

Our Mission

Nurturing Communities Through Compassionate Excellence

At A Touch Of Mercy, LLC, our mission is to compassionately serve and uplift individuals and communities. We are dedicated to providing exceptional services, whether in transportation, behavioral health, or education, with a focus on fostering holistic well-being. Our commitment extends beyond business to actively contribute to community welfare, promoting health, education, and positive connections. Grounded in a legacy of care and guided by unwavering faith, we strive to make a lasting, positive impact on the lives we touch, embodying the essence of mercy in all what we do.
